Animal shelters have recorded the details of their furry friend's needs
How many pets are far from home or in need of a home? Is age a significant contributing factor to their status? Animal shelters address these issues by keeping records of the animals they have kept track of. This data has been drawn from the records of Washington state's animal shelters.
